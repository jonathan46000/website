Toner on board

PCB Making

I've had experience in two methods of making printed circuit boards, a toner transfer process and a photographic process. With toner transfer heat is used to transfer printer toner onto a copper PCB to protect the copper you want to keep from washing away in acid. In the photograpic process a transparency and light sensative film are used to achieve this.
