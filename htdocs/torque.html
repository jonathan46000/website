Loader in action with wrench

Torque loader project and the lab I worked at

I designed and built this torque loader to load torque wrenches at a metrology lab I worked at. It allows you to easily apply any desired torque to the wrench while it's attached to the transducer. This saved the company about $10,000. Other photos of the lab are also on this page.
