Industrial Dead Weight Tester Software

The average person would be surprised to find out how precision pressure devices are calibrated and what kind of effort goes into to verifying the accuracy of the measurement. Pressure balances or industrial dead weight tester operate on a simple principle that a mass exerting a force over the area of a floating pistion will exert an exact pressure. However, a lot more goes into it than that and I wrote this software suite to simplify the math and overtime it also took over a lot of other calculations performed at the lab.

Density of the air which is dependent on current atomspheric pressure and humidity affect the force exerted. So does temperature, local gravity, and the internal pressure being exerted itself. All of these factors become cumbersome to calculate by hand. That is why I originally wrote this software and later included modules for thermodynamic calculations for some of our other calibration systems as well.
